About

We focus on creative design solutions for complex buildings.

We are a team of creative, precise, detail-oriented professionals with a belief that deep subject-matter expertise and the use of the latest technologies can aid in understanding and solving hard design problems. As building systems and regulatory requirements become increasingly complex, the need for knowledgeable consultants grows in parallel. We work collaboratively with our clients, project stakeholders, and diverse consultant teams to deliver holistic solutions for our clients.

We excel in the delivery of science & technology, healthcare, academic, and industrial projects. We have specific expertise in projects incorporating laboratories, cleanrooms, and other highly controlled spaces. Our portfolio consists of projects for diverse industries such as pharmaceutical manufacturing, compliance testing, specialty packaging, and secure product storage. We also have deep expertise in the healthcare sector, including the delivery of complex hospital renovations, long-term care, and seniors living projects.
