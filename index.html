About

We focus on creative design solutions for complex buildings.

Section Architects is a full-service architectural practice with a focus on creative design solutions for complex buildings. Led by Dave Saunders and Mike Nally, the firm takes a rigorous, concept-driven approach to design for healthcare, technology, laboratory, hospitality and academic clients.

Our core strength is our ability to navigate and manage complexity. Through years of experience managing difficult approvals processes, large consultant teams and diverse stakeholders, we have developed a design approach built on technical expertise and strong project management.
