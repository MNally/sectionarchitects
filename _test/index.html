About

We've been helping our clients design and deliver complex buildings for a decade.

Section Architects is a full-service architectural practice founded by Dave Saunders and Mike Nally in 2019. We are precise, detail-oriented professionals with a belief that the blending of design and technology can aid in understanding and solving hard architectural problems.

Our core strength is our ability to navigate and manage complexity. While our portfolio covers many sectors and building types, we excel in the delivery of challenging projects for healthcare, technology, laboratory, hospitality and educational clients. As building systems become increasingly complex, the need for knowledgeable consultants grows in parallel. We use design-thinking and technology to understand and solve hard design problems in collaboration with our clients.
